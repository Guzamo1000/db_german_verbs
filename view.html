Conjugation of German verb umziehen

The conjugation of the verb umziehen (move, change lodgings) is irregular. Basic forms are zieht um, zog um and ist umgezogen. The stem vowels are ie - o - o. The auxiliary verb of umziehen is sein. haben can be used as well. First syllable um- of umziehen is separable. Can also be used not separable. The flection is in Active and the use as Main. For a better understanding, countless examples of the verb umziehen are available. For practicing and consolidating, there are also free worksheets for umziehen. You can not just umziehen conjugate, but all German verbs. The verb is part of the thesaurus of Zertifikat Deutsch respectivly Level A1. Comments ☆

um·ziehen

zieht um · zog um · ist umgezogen

 move, change lodgings, change one's place, change one's quarters, change one's residence, flit, move flat, migrate

den Wohnsitz wechseln; übersiedeln; gehen; (den) Anbieter wechseln; zügeln; wechseln zu

(acc., zu+D)
